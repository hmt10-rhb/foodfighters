Rango Packs

Bigger packs give you more rolls — every roll uses the exact same rarity odds regardless of pack size.

Fusão Culinária

9 same-rarity Rangos → 1 fusion attempt. Success upgrades to the next rarity — with a small chance of a +2 tier jump. Failure still returns 1 fresh Rango of the same rarity, so an attempt is never a total wipe. The higher the tier, the lower the odds (shown per row). Auto-fuse picks your 9 lowest-level Rangos so your invested ones stay safe. Only Receita de Vó can't be fused further.

Manual select

Prefer to choose exactly which Rangos get fused? Pick 9 below.

🧪 Lab

Hero-modification systems: spend currency, materials or other heroes to reshape one you want to invest in.

🎲 Stat Re-roll

Re-roll a hero's power/range/speed within its own rarity band. Cost scales with rarity and level. Keeps identity, rarity, level, skills and any permanent Sacrifice/Ascension bonuses.

💀 Sacrifice XP Dust

Burn owned heroes into permanent power for ONE chosen hero. Never changes rarity — that's what Fusion is for.

👶 Nursery Breeding

Pick 2 parents to breed 1 child. Child rarity is always the LOWER of the two parents' — breeding never upgrades rarity, only Fusion does. Phantom/Swift have a real chance to inherit; Midas/Cataclysm can never be inherited.

🔮 Skill Implant

Spend Skill Shards (dropped rarely — 0.3% per crate, 0.5% per chest destroyed) to attempt implanting Phantom or Swift on a hero who doesn't have it. Shards are consumed either way. Midas/Cataclysm can never be implanted.

🌌 Ascension

Any Epic+ hero at max level (10) can consume 3 OTHER heroes of the same rarity to Ascend, gaining a permanent stacking mineRate bonus (diminishing per extra ascension on that hero). The main endgame sink once you're out of other things to spend heroes on.
